Mercenery

The Mercenary deals fast damage while dodging incoming threats. Whirlwind can be used to stay in mid-air longer. Fit skills between Blinding Assaults to maximize time spent invincible.

Acrid

Acrid deals huge amounts of damage after stacking poisons.Try to stun targets inside your Caustic Sludge for maximum damage. Remember that you can fight at both melee and range!

Chefp

The CHEF is extremely proficient at dealing with large groups of customers. Combining a delicious Glaze with a finishing Sear is sure to dazzle even the toughest of crowds!

Artificer

The Artificer uses her elemental abilities to defeat her enemies with precision. By raising Frost Barriers, she's able to keep foes at bay in order to prepare Charged Nanobombs, or to escape into the optimal ranger for her flurry of Flame Chackrams.
